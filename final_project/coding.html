Me & Code

How I stated coding...

When I was 11, me and my friends were really interested in computers. It turned out that one of my friends was already going to some academy to learn how to code. I asked him if he minds if I and my friends will go there too, and he did not mind. After a few days I told about this my friends and all of us registered to "Hello IT" .

We all started learning how to create websites, but the information that they taught was very basic. It was not enough for me and I started to do more research on academies that teach kids how to code. It was already Summer, and my mom found out that the university in our city offered Scratch courses for kids. I registered for it, started building basic games using Scratch .

After this Summer, our middle school started to teach us Unity. All of us were very excited, but this did not last too long, as our school was in quarantine. I remember that I stopped coding until the pandemic ended. Then when another Summer came, I started studying Python Programming language at "Bobir Akilkhanov Academy Napa Edy Hub". Before I was only learning basic programming languages so when it came to learning Python, it was very hard.

By the time I was studying Python, we got our visa to the United States! We moved there, and I stopped programming again for 5 months. After this, I asked my mom to buy me a set of courses from Mosh Hamedani as a New Year gift. I started doing one course by one, and still doing them nowadays. I received a couple of certificates and learned even more.

When I was a sophomore in High School, I enrolled myself in INTRO to Programming. There we learned HTML&CSS and Scratch. This was easy for me as I already had a base knowledge. When the next Summer came, I started taking CCP classes at Sincalir Community College. The first course that I took was CIS 1111 C++ programming language. It was simmilar to Python, but the syntax was more complex. I successfully finished that course too.

In my Junior year of high school, I am now taking CIS1350 HTML&CSS Web Development. This class in depth taught me web development, and I realized how much content there is in web development.

These are the certificates that I received from Code With Mosh
