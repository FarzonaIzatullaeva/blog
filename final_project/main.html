My passions are computers and Artificial Intelligence (AI). As a high school student, I study basic programming languages. As I will go to the University, I will study computer science field in depth, and accomplish all of the projects that I am passionate about!


In my free time, I like to think of different projects that I would like to code, and do courses that help me to gain the right knowledge. In addition to that, I also value my family time and try to spend time with my relatives, and play with my cat Barsik.


I love moments even small ones that make me happy like a hot chocolate on a rainy day, or even typing this text that you are reading right now. I get motivation from my improvements and results. Sometimes it might be very hard to keep up and maybe even see the results that I did not expect to have, but taking a moment and talking with my mom about problems that I have and listening to her advice helps me a lot. In the end I try to fix the situation, learn from that case, and continue to move on...
