Me & Vlog

How I stated my YouTube channel...

When I was a child, my mom introduced me to YouTube. At that time I did not know how it worked, and I thought that it only had videos about Back to School. I remember that I watched almost all of the Back to School videos that were on YouTube back in that time. Then I liked to imagine a camera that filmed me, and I imagined that I was a Youtuber.

Well, these imaginations came true! I started learning more about YouTube and started watching videoes on how to start filming, uploading videos, editing, and so on. I was very interested in vlogging. I liked to film study-with-me videoes and day-in-the-life vlogs.

When we moved to the United States, I started filming my life in the United States. In my home country, people like to watch these kinds of videos, and I like to film them. The language that I run my YouTube channel is Russian, so if you understand Russian you can watch my videoes.
